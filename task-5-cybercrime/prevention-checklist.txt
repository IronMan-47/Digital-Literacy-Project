                    Prevention Checklist
🛡️ Stay Safe Online
-> Do not approve any payment request unless you clearly know who sent it
-> Always double-check the name and details before making any transaction
-> Never share your OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank
-> Avoid clicking on random links received through messages or emails
-> Try to use only trusted and official apps for online payments
-> Keep your phone and apps updated to avoid security risks
-> Be careful while using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
-> If something feels suspicious, it’s better to stop and verify first
___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

💰 UPI Safety (Important)
-> Remember that approving a UPI request means money will be debited, not received
-> Always read the payment message carefully before confirming
-> Do not scan QR codes from unknown or unverified sources
___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

📞 In Case of Fraud
-> Immediately report the issue on cybercrime.gov.in
-> Call the national cybercrime helpline 1930 as soon as possible
-> Inform your bank quickly to prevent further loss
